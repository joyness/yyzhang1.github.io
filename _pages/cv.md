Biography
======
* Yuanyuan Zhang received her Ph.D degree in Traffic Information Engineering and Control from Shanghai Maritime University in 2023. From 2021 to 2022, she was a visiting Ph.D. Student in Electrical and Computer Engineering with the University of Victoria, Canada. She is currently a lecturer with the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Changzhou Institute of Technology. Her research interests include wireless sensor networks, underwater acoustic sensor networks, localization and tracking, Intelligent prediction and deep learning, and so on.
